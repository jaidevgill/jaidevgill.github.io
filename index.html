Jaidev Gill

I am a second year PhD student in the department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor working with Professor Lisa Li. Some of my research interests include network control theory, system identification, and nonlinear systems theory.

I'm a recent graduate of the Engineering Physics program at the University of British Columbia. During my time at UBC I worked on understanding the learned representations of neural networks with Professor Christos Thrampoulidis.
